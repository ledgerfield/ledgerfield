Accounting

6 definitions. Every term links to related concepts and, where it touches the mesh, to the underlying knitweb / P2P primitives.

Chart of accounts #

The account structure of the ledger. LedgerField ships NL (RGS), UK (FRS 102) and US (GAAP) charts, plus country schemas for 100+ jurisdictions.

Related: Journal entry · Trial balance

Journal entry #

A dated line with a debit account, a credit account and an amount — the atom of double-entry bookkeeping.

Related: Chart of accounts · Trial balance

Trial balance #

The per-account sum of debits and credits; it must balance before you close a period.

Related: Journal entry · Profit & loss (Winst & Verlies)

Profit & loss (Winst & Verlies) #

Revenue minus costs over a period. LedgerField derives it directly from your journal entries.

Related: Trial balance

Entity / holding structure #

A company in your structure (BV, Holding, ZZP, Ltd, GmbH…), optionally nested under a parent. Reports and filings are scoped per entity.

Related: Chart of accounts

SAF-T export #

Standard Audit File for Tax — an XML export of your ledger for tax authorities. Deterministically generated from the same canonical data model.

Related: Journal entry
