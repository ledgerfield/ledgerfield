Taxation

9 definitions. Every term links to related concepts and, where it touches the mesh, to the underlying knitweb / P2P primitives.

VPB — NL corporate income tax #

Vennootschapsbelasting: 19% up to €200k taxable profit, 25.8% above (2025). Built-in calculator with effective-rate output.

Related: IB — NL personal income tax · CIT — corporate income tax (global) · Effective tax rate

IB — NL personal income tax #

Inkomstenbelasting across Box 1 (work), Box 2 (substantial-interest dividend) and Box 3 (wealth), with the 2025 brackets and credits.

Related: VPB — NL corporate income tax · DGA gebruikelijk loon

WBSO — R&D payroll credit #

A Dutch payroll-tax reduction on qualifying R&D wage costs (32%, or 40% for starters, on the first band). Modelled in the tax tab.

Related: VPB — NL corporate income tax

DGA gebruikelijk loon #

The customary-salary rule for director-shareholders. The 2025 benchmark (ijkpunt) is €41,138; the app flags salaries below the norm as a correction risk.

Related: IB — NL personal income tax

BTW — VAT #

Value-added tax (21% standard NL). Quarterly return helper computes VAT charged minus deductible input VAT.

Related: VPB — NL corporate income tax

CIT — corporate income tax (global) #

Corporate income tax modelled for 100+ jurisdictions from CID-addressed rulesets, each with its own brackets, entity types and 2025 rates.

Related: VPB — NL corporate income tax · Ruleset · Effective tax rate
On the knitweb: CID — Content Identifier

Effective tax rate #

Tax actually payable divided by the taxable base — the number that matters for benchmarking, and one of the seven shareable aggregate categories.

Related: CIT — corporate income tax (global) · VPB — NL corporate income tax · Data categories (7)

Ruleset #

A jurisdiction's tax parameters as a canonical JSON, addressed by CID and shared peer-to-peer over gossip. Rates are public-domain, so rulesets are safe to replicate; you verify one by recomputing its CID.

Related: CIT — corporate income tax (global) · BTW — VAT · VPB — NL corporate income tax
On the knitweb: CID — Content Identifier · Gossip / anti-entropy · Canonical encoding

Payroll (loonstrook) #

Gross-to-net payslip generation with employer burden (pension, WW, WIA, ZVW), plus the DGA customary-salary check.

Related: DGA gebruikelijk loon
