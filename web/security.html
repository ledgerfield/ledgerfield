Security & responsible disclosure

LedgerField handles financial data, so we take security seriously. If you find a vulnerability, please report it privately and give us a chance to fix it before public disclosure.

Report a vulnerability: open a private advisory at GitHub Security Advisories. Machine-readable contact: security.txt.

In scope

  • The app (ledgerfield.html) — vault encryption, local storage, calculators, exports.
  • Tax rulesets and their CID verification.
  • The k-anonymity guarantees of the data market.
  • This website.

Out of scope

  • Social engineering, phishing and physical attacks.
  • Denial of service / volumetric attacks.
  • Findings that require an already-compromised device (the vault trusts the local machine).

Our posture

Offline by default

The app runs client-side; raw data lives only in your encrypted vault. No server, no telemetry.

Verify, don’t trust

Rulesets and market packages are content-addressed — you verify them by recomputing their CID.

Privacy floor

Only anonymised k≥5 aggregates ever leave the device.

Thank you for helping keep LedgerField users safe.
