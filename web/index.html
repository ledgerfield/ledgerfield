Why LedgerField

🔒

Encrypted vault

All your books are stored in an encrypted vault in the browser. Set a password, lock it when you step away, and export an encrypted backup — your raw data never leaves the device.

📚

Double-entry bookkeeping

Journal entries against a full chart of accounts — NL RGS, UK FRS102, or US GAAP. Import via CSV, view a Trial Balance, and generate a Profit & Loss report in seconds.

📈

NL tax engine + 100+ country CIT

Built-in 2025 rates for VPB, IB (Box 1/2/3), WBSO, DGA customary-salary, and BTW. Corporate income tax rulesets for 100+ jurisdictions, fetched and verified by CID over the peer network.

💰

Payroll & payslips

Enter salary, commute km, WFH days, and pension contribution. The app computes gross pay, 8% holiday pay, travel and WFH allowances, wage tax, ZVW, and full employer costs — one click per worker per period.

🔁

P2P ruleset sync via knitweb

Import a peer's tax ruleset and verify it by recomputing its content ID — no trust required. Publish your own rulesets. The public relay is 5mart.ml; the app works fully offline between syncs.

📊

Anonymised data market (k ≥ 5)

Opt in per category and jurisdiction. Aggregates are only publishable when at least 5 distinct nodes contribute — no individual trace is ever exposed. Browse, buy, or earn from published packages.

How it works

  1. Keep your books offline. Open app.html in any modern browser, set up your entity (BV, ZZP, LLC, GmbH, and more), and start posting journal entries. Everything is encrypted locally — no account, no server, no sync required.
  2. The app computes taxes, payslips, and filings. Switch to the Taxes tab for VPB, IB, WBSO, DGA, and BTW calculations. Open Payroll to generate payslips. Go to Filing to preview your VPB/IB/BTW forms and export JSON or SAF-T XML.
  3. Optionally share only public artefacts over the knitweb. Publish or import CID-addressed tax rulesets and anonymised data-market aggregates via the P2P tab. Raw books stay on your device — always.

Privacy model

Your raw books never leave your device. LedgerField is offline-first by design: the entire app is a single HTML file that runs in your browser with no network calls. The vault encrypts every record with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 before writing it to browser storage. The only data that ever traverses the network are content-addressed tax rulesets (public by definition) and fully anonymised, k ≥ 5 aggregates — both published by you, on your terms, via the knitweb P2P layer.
