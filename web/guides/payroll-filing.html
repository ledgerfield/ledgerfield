Payroll & filing

Calculate gross-to-net payslips with full employer costs, then generate and export your VPB, IB, and BTW returns — entirely offline.

Tab 4 — Payroll Tab 5 — Filing NL 2025

1. Payroll — Add a Worker and Generate a Payslip

The Payroll tab (tab 4) takes a worker's inputs and produces a full gross-to-net payslip plus a breakdown of total employer cost.

Add a Worker

  1. Open Tab 4 — Payroll and click Add Worker.
  2. Enter the worker's name and monthly gross salary.
  3. Set commute distance (km per day) and WFH days per week.
  4. Enter the employee pension contribution as a percentage.
  5. Click Calculate — the payslip generates instantly in your browser.

2. Filing — VPB, IB, BTW and Exports

Tab 5 (Filing) pre-fills return forms from your ledger data. You can review figures, check deadlines, and export structured files for submission or audit — all without an internet connection.

Available Returns

VPB — Corporate Tax

Vennootschapsbelasting. 19% up to €200k profit, 25.8% above. Figures are pre-filled from your Profit & Loss statement.

IB — Income Tax

Inkomstenbelasting for DGA and ZZP. Box 1, Box 2, and Box 3 breakdown with applicable credits (AHK, AK).

BTW — VAT

Omzetbelasting at 21% standard rate. Quarterly returns are pre-filled automatically from your journal entries.

Deadlines

ReturnPeriodTypical deadline
BTWQuarterlyLast day of the month following the quarter end
VPBFinancial year5 months after financial year end
IBCalendar year1 May of the following year

Export a Return

  1. Open Tab 5 — Filing and select the return type: VPB, IB, or BTW.
  2. Review the pre-filled figures — drawn directly from your ledger.
  3. Check the deadlines panel and confirm the filing period.
  4. Click Export JSON for a portable, machine-readable archive.
  5. Click Export SAF-T XML to produce a SAF-T-compliant audit file for tax authority submission.

JSON export

Human-readable and portable. Hand it to your accountant, keep it as an archive, or re-import it into LedgerField on another device.

SAF-T XML export

Standardised audit file format. Accepted by the Belastingdienst and most EU tax authorities for audit trail and e-filing purposes.

Both exports are generated locally in your browser. Your raw financial data never reaches a server at any point.
